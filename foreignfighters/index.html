Foreign Fighter Project

About the Foreign Fighter Project

In a study led by CSIS Transnational Threats Project, Foreign Fighters investigates how foreign fighters have shaped the conflicts to which they travel and explores where they may go next. The study draws a combination of on-the-ground field interviews and cutting edge research to explain nascent trends in foreign fighter behavior. Learn more here.
